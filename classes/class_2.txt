Pasta

1: pasta_type (string)
2: sauce_type (string)
3: vegetarian_option (boolean)
4: allergen_alert (boolean, string)

1: rotini (selects rotini as pasta_type)
2: marinara_sauce (selects marinara_sauce as sauce_type)
3: vegetarian (switches vegetarian_option boolean to true)
4: cheese_allergy (switches allergen_alert boolean to true, then as a comment the item that is an allergy.)

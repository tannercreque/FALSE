Pizza (class)

1: Crust (string)
2: Toppings (string)
3: time_due (datetime)
4: marinara_sauce (boolean)

2: use_alfredo (changes marinara_sauce to false)
3: handtossed_dough (determines crust type (string))
4: pepperoni_add (adds an item to the toppings string)
